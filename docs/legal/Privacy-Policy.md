## 1. Introduction

### 1.1 About BelieversFlow

BelieversFlow ("we," "us," or "our") is a Christian productivity application that helps users manage tasks, track prayer life, read the Bible, journal, access hymns, and receive AI-powered faith guidance. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices.

### 1.2 Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to:
- The BelieversFlow web application (believers-flow-frontend.vercel.app)
- The BelieversFlow Android application
- All related services and features

### 1.3 Our Privacy Principles

We operate under the following principles:

| Principle | Our Commitment |
|---|---|
| **Privacy by Design** | Privacy is built into our architecture from the ground up |
| **Privacy by Default** | We collect the minimum data necessary for functionality |
| **Data Minimization** | We only collect what we need, nothing more |
| **Transparency** | We clearly explain what we collect and why |
| **User Control** | You can access, export, and delete your data at any time |
| **No Ads** | We never display advertisements |
| **No Data Selling** | We never sell your personal information |

### 1.4 Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data based on:
- **Consent:** When you explicitly agree to data collection
- **Contract:** When processing is necessary to provide our services
- **Legitimate Interest:** When processing is necessary for our legitimate business interests
- **Legal Obligation:** When we must comply with applicable laws

## 4. Data Storage & Security

### 4.1 Data Storage

| Data Type | Storage Location | Encryption |
|---|---|---|
| Local app data | Your device only | Device encryption |
| Account data | PostgreSQL (Aiven) | TLS in transit, encrypted at rest |
| Synced data | PostgreSQL (Aiven) | TLS in transit, encrypted at rest |
| Bible embeddings | Pinecone | Encrypted at rest |
| AI conversations | Not stored server-side | N/A |

### 4.2 Security Measures

We implement the following security measures:

| Measure | Implementation |
|---|---|
| **Encryption in Transit** | TLS 1.2+ for all communications |
| **Encryption at Rest** | AES-256 for stored data |
| **Password Hashing** | bcrypt with salt |
| **Authentication** | JWT tokens with 30-day expiry |
| **API Security** | Rate limiting, input validation |
| **Database Security** | SSL connections, parameterized queries |
| **Access Control** | Role-based access control |
| **Audit Logging** | Security event logging |

### 4.3 Data Breach Response

In the event of a data breach:
1. We will notify affected users within 72 hours
2. We will notify relevant authorities as required by law
3. We will document the breach and our response
4. We will implement measures to prevent recurrence

## 7. Your Rights

### 7.1 Right to Access

You have the right to request:
- A copy of all personal data we hold about you
- Information about how your data is processed
- Details of third parties who receive your data

**How to exercise:** Use the Privacy Dashboard in-app or contact us.

### 7.2 Right to Deletion

You have the right to request:
- Deletion of your personal data
- Deletion of your account
- Removal from our systems

**Exceptions:** We may retain data required by law or for legitimate business purposes (e.g., transaction records for 7 years).

**How to exercise:** Use the Privacy Dashboard in-app or contact us.

### 7.3 Right to Rectification

You have the right to:
- Correct inaccurate personal data
- Update incomplete information

**How to exercise:** Update your profile in-app settings.

### 7.4 Right to Portability

You have the right to:
- Export your data in a machine-readable format (JSON)
- Transfer your data to another service

**How to exercise:** Use the "Export Data" feature in Settings > Backup.

### 7.5 Right to Object

You have the right to:
- Object to processing based on legitimate interest
- Opt out of anonymized data collection
- Opt out of marketing communications

### 7.6 Right to Restrict Processing

You have the right to request that we:
- Limit how we use your data
- Stop processing while accuracy is verified
- Stop processing while objection is considered

### 7.7 Right to Withdraw Consent

You have the right to:
- Withdraw consent at any time
- Delete your account and all associated data
- Disable cloud sync

**How to exercise:** Use account settings or contact us.

### 7.8 Exercising Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights:
1. Use the Privacy Dashboard in-app
2. Email: privacy@believersflow.app
3. GitHub: github.com/ecoinboxhub/Christian_task_manager/issues

We will respond to all requests within 30 days.
