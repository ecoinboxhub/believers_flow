## 1. Introduction

### 1.1 Purpose

These Community Guidelines explain how users are expected to interact with BelieversFlow and each other. They help maintain a positive, respectful, and supportive community for all believers.

### 1.2 Scope

These guidelines apply to:
- All users of BelieversFlow
- All content created or shared through the app
- All interactions with other users
- All use of AI features
- All community features (when available)

### 1.3 Christian Community

BelieversFlow is a Christian application. While we welcome users of all backgrounds, we ask that all users respect our Christian values and community standards. We aim to create a space where believers can grow in their faith together.

## 2. Our Community Values

### 2.1 Core Values

We uphold these core values based on Biblical principles:

| Value | Description | Scripture |
|---|---|---|
| **Love** | Treat others with love and compassion | John 13:34 |
| **Respect** | Show respect to all users | Romans 12:10 |
| **Kindness** | Be kind in all interactions | Ephesians 4:32 |
| **Forgiveness** | Show grace when others make mistakes | Colossians 3:13 |
| **Truth** | Be honest and truthful | Ephesians 4:15 |
| **Encouragement** | Build others up, not tear them down | 1 Thessalonians 5:11 |
| **Humility** | Approach discussions with humility | Philippians 2:3 |
| **Unity** | Focus on unity in Christ | Ephesians 4:3 |

### 2.2 The Golden Rule

> "So in everything, do to others what you would have them do to you." — Matthew 7:12

This principle guides all interactions in our community.

## 3. Acceptable Behavior

### 3.1 Positive Interactions

We encourage:
- **Encouraging others:** Share words of encouragement and support
- **Praying for others:** Offer to pray for fellow believers
- **Sharing testimonies:** Share how God is working in your life
- **Asking questions:** Seek understanding and growth
- **Sharing resources:** Share helpful Christian resources
- **Celebrating together:** Celebrate answered prayers and blessings

### 3.2 Respectful Communication

- Be kind and courteous in all interactions
- Listen to others with empathy
- Acknowledge different perspectives
- Avoid being judgmental
- Speak the truth in love (Ephesians 4:15)

### 3.3 Constructive Engagement

- Offer helpful, constructive feedback
- Focus on the issue, not the person
- Acknowledge when you're wrong
- Be open to learning and growth
- Show grace to those who disagree

## 4. Prohibited Behavior

### 4.1 Hate Speech

You must not post content that:
- Attacks or demeans any group based on race, ethnicity, religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or other protected characteristics
- Promotes violence or discrimination
- Uses slurs or derogatory language
- Incites hatred or intolerance

### 4.2 Harassment

You must not:
- Bully or intimidate other users
- Send unwanted messages
- Stalk or harass others
- Make threats or engage in threatening behavior
- Engage in personal attacks
- Dox or share private information

### 4.3 Spam

You must not:
- Post repetitive or irrelevant content
- Send unsolicited advertisements
- Flood the service with messages
- Create multiple accounts to evade restrictions
- Use automated tools to post content

### 4.4 Misinformation

You must not:
- Share false or misleading information
- Spread conspiracy theories
- Present opinion as fact
- Misrepresent Scripture
- Create or share fake news

### 4.5 Controversial Content

We ask that users avoid:
- Divisive political discussions
- Controversial theological debates that may cause division
- Judgmental or condemnatory content
- Arguments that do not edify the community

### 4.6 Impersonation

You must not:
- Impersonate other users
- Impersonate BelieversFlow staff
- Create fake accounts
- Misrepresent your identity

## 5. Content Standards

### 5.1 Content Requirements

All content shared through BelieversFlow must:
- Be appropriate for a Christian audience
- Comply with these Community Guidelines
- Respect the dignity of all people
- Be truthful and accurate
- Not infringe on others' rights
- Be edifying and constructive

### 5.2 Language Standards

- Use respectful, wholesome language
- Avoid profanity, vulgarity, or obscenity
- Avoid taking the Lord's name in vain
- Use language that builds up, not tears down

### 5.3 Theological Content

When sharing theological content:
- Share your perspective with humility
- Acknowledge that others may disagree
- Avoid claiming absolute authority on debatable topics
- Focus on unity in Christ
- Reference Scripture appropriately
- Respect denominational differences

### 5.4 Prayer Requests

When sharing prayer requests:
- Share only what you're comfortable with others seeing
- Do not include sensitive personal information of others
- Respect the privacy of those you're praying for
- Understand that prayer requests may be visible to others
- Be sensitive to the needs of others

## 6. Prayer Requests

### 6.1 Guidelines for Sharing

Prayer requests are a sacred part of our community. When sharing:
- Share from the heart
- Be honest about your needs
- Respect the privacy of others
- Be sensitive to others' struggles
- Follow up with praise reports when appropriate

### 6.2 Privacy Considerations

- Prayer requests may be visible to other users
- Do not share others' personal information without consent
- Be cautious about sharing sensitive details
- You can delete prayer requests at any time

### 6.3 Responding to Prayer Requests

- Respond with love and compassion
- Offer to pray for the person
- Avoid being judgmental or preachy
- Respect different perspectives
- Follow up when appropriate

## 7. AI Usage Guidelines

### 7.1 Appropriate Use

AI features are designed for:
- Faith-related questions
- Bible study assistance
- Prayer guidance
- Personal productivity
- Spiritual growth

### 7.2 Inappropriate Use

You must not use AI to:
- Generate harmful or dangerous content
- Create false or misleading information
- Harass or bully others
- Engage in illegal activities
- Circumvent safety measures
- Generate spam or unsolicited content

### 7.3 AI Limitations

Understand that:
- AI responses may contain errors
- AI is not a substitute for professional advice
- AI does not have personal experiences or emotions
- AI should not be relied upon for critical decisions

### 7.4 Reporting AI Issues

If AI generates inappropriate content:
- Do not share or distribute it
- Report it immediately
- We will investigate and take action
- We may improve our systems based on your feedback

## 8. Music & Hymn Usage

### 8.1 Permitted Use

You may:
- Listen to hymn melodies for personal enjoyment
- Sing along for personal worship
- Use hymns in personal devotions
- Share hymn lyrics with others (for personal use)

### 8.2 Restrictions

You must not:
- Distribute hymn audio files
- Use hymns for commercial purposes
- Modify or create derivative works
- Claim ownership of hymn content
- Use hymns in public performances without permission

### 8.3 Copyright

Hymn lyrics and melodies are protected by copyright. While we provide access for personal use, you must respect copyright laws.

## 9. Reporting Violations

### 9.1 How to Report

If you see content or behavior that violates these guidelines:
1. Use the in-app "Report" feature (when available)
2. Email: report@believersflow.app
3. GitHub: github.com/ecoinboxhub/believers_flow/issues

### 9.2 What to Include

When reporting, please include:
- Description of the violation
- Username or identifier (if known)
- Screenshots or evidence (if available)
- Your contact information (optional)

### 9.3 Confidentiality

Reports are kept confidential. We will not share your identity with the reported user.

### 9.4 Response Time

We aim to respond to reports within:
- **Critical violations (illegal content):** 24 hours
- **Serious violations (harassment, hate speech):** 48 hours
- **Other violations:** 7 days

## 10. Enforcement

### 10.1 Enforcement Actions

Depending on the severity of the violation, we may:

| Action | Description |
|---|---|
| **Warning** | Notification that behavior is unacceptable |
| **Content Removal** | Removal of violating content |
| **Feature Restriction** | Temporary loss of access to certain features |
| **Account Suspension** | Temporary account deactivation |
| **Account Termination** | Permanent account deletion |
| **Legal Action** | Referral to law enforcement (for illegal content) |

### 10.2 Severity Levels

| Level | Examples | Typical Response |
|---|---|---|
| **Minor** | Spam, minor etiquette violations | Warning |
| **Moderate** | Harassment, copyright infringement | Content removal, feature restriction |
| **Serious** | Hate speech, threats, impersonation | Account suspension |
| **Critical** | Illegal content, CSAM, terrorism | Account termination, legal action |

### 10.3 Appeals

If you believe an action was taken in error:
1. Contact support@believersflow.app within 30 days
2. Explain your perspective
3. We will review your case
4. Our decision is final
