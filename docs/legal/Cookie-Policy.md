## 1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website or use an application. They help us recognize your device and remember certain information about your visit.

### 1.1 What Cookies Are Not

Cookies are not:
- Programs that can run code
- Viruses or malware
- Used to access files on your device
- Used to identify you personally (unless you provide that information)

## 5. Managing Cookies

### 5.1 Browser Settings

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings:

- **Chrome:** Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies
- **Safari:** Preferences > Privacy > Cookies
- **Firefox:** Options > Privacy & Security > Cookies
- **Edge:** Settings > Privacy, Search, and Services > Cookies

### 5.2 App Settings

You can manage some cookie preferences through the app settings:

- **Notifications:** Settings > Notifications
- **Theme:** Settings > Theme
- **Language:** Settings > Language

### 5.3 Opting Out

You can opt out of analytics by:
- Using the "Do Not Track" setting in your browser
- Contacting us at privacy@believersflow.app

### 5.4 Disabling Cookies

Disabling certain cookies may affect the app's functionality:
- **Essential cookies:** Cannot be disabled
- **Functional cookies:** Disabling may affect personalization
- **Analytics cookies:** Disabling does not affect functionality
