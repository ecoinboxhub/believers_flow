## 1. Introduction

### 1.1 Purpose

This Content Moderation Policy explains how BelieversFlow moderates content to maintain a safe, respectful, and supportive Christian community.

### 1.2 Scope

This policy applies to:
- User-generated content (tasks, diary entries, prayer requests)
- AI-generated content
- Community features (when available)
- User profiles
- Any content shared through the service

### 1.3 Our Commitment

We are committed to:
- Maintaining a safe and supportive environment
- Respecting user expression while protecting the community
- Applying moderation consistently and fairly
- Being transparent about our moderation practices
- Providing mechanisms for appeal

## 2. Content Standards

### 2.1 Quality Standards

All content should be:
- **Appropriate:** Suitable for a Christian audience
- **Respectful:** Treat others with dignity and respect
- **Truthful:** Be honest and accurate
- **Edifying:** Build up the community
- **Constructive:** Contribute positively to discussions

### 2.2 Language Standards

Content should:
- Use respectful, wholesome language
- Avoid profanity, vulgarity, or obscenity
- Avoid taking the Lord's name in vain
- Be free from hate speech or discriminatory language

### 2.3 Theological Standards

When sharing theological content:
- Share with humility
- Acknowledge different perspectives
- Avoid divisive debates
- Focus on unity in Christ
- Reference Scripture appropriately

## 3. Prohibited Content

### 3.1 Illegal Content

The following content is strictly prohibited:
- Content that violates any applicable law
- Child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
- Terrorist content
- Content promoting violence or illegal activities
- Content violating copyright or intellectual property rights

### 3.2 Hate Content

The following content is prohibited:
- Content that attacks or demeans any group based on race, ethnicity, religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or other protected characteristics
- Content promoting violence or discrimination
- Content using slurs or derogatory language
- Content inciting hatred or intolerance

### 3.3 Harassment Content

The following content is prohibited:
- Content that bullies or intimidates other users
- Content that stalks or harasses others
- Content making threats or engaging in threatening behavior
- Content engaging in personal attacks
- Content doxing or sharing private information

### 3.4 Spam Content

The following content is prohibited:
- Repetitive or irrelevant content
- Unsolicited advertisements
- Flooding the service with messages
- Content from fake or impersonated accounts
- Content from automated tools

### 3.5 Misinformation Content

The following content is prohibited:
- False or misleading information
- Conspiracy theories
- Content presenting opinion as fact
- Content misrepresenting Scripture
- Fake news or disinformation

### 3.6 Harmful Content

The following content is prohibited:
- Content promoting self-harm or suicide
- Content promoting eating disorders
- Content promoting violence or dangerous activities
- Content containing graphic or disturbing material
- Content promoting substance abuse

### 3.7 Sexual Content

The following content is prohibited:
- Sexually explicit content
- Pornographic content
- Content promoting sexual exploitation
- Content containing nudity
- Content promoting sexual misconduct

## 4. Moderation Process

### 4.1 Automated Moderation

We use automated systems to:
- Detect spam and abuse
- Filter prohibited content
- Identify potential violations
- Flag content for review

### 4.2 Human Moderation

Human moderators review content to:
- Verify automated decisions
- Handle complex cases
- Make final determinations
- Ensure consistency

### 4.3 User Reporting

Users can report content by:
- Using the in-app report feature (when available)
- Emailing report@believersflow.app
- Submitting a GitHub issue

### 4.4 Review Process

When content is reported or flagged:
1. Content is reviewed within 24-48 hours
2. A determination is made based on this policy
3. Action is taken if necessary
4. The user is notified of the decision
5. The user may appeal if they disagree

## 5. Enforcement Actions

### 5.1 Warning

A warning is issued for:
- Minor violations
- First-time offenses
- Accidental violations

The user is notified and the content may be removed.

### 5.2 Content Removal

Content may be removed for:
- Violations of this policy
- Inappropriate content
- Copyright infringement
- User request

The user is notified and may appeal.

### 5.3 Feature Restriction

Features may be restricted for:
- Repeated violations
- Serious violations
- Abuse of features

The user retains access to other features.

### 5.4 Account Suspension

Accounts may be suspended for:
- Serious or repeated violations
- Harassment or bullying
- Spam or abuse

The account is temporarily deactivated.

### 5.5 Account Termination

Accounts may be terminated for:
- Illegal content
- Severe violations
- Repeated serious violations

The account is permanently deleted.

## 6. Appeals Process

### 6.1 How to Appeal

If you believe a moderation action was taken in error:
1. Contact support@believersflow.app within 30 days
2. Explain why you believe the action was incorrect
3. Provide any supporting evidence
4. We will review your appeal

### 6.2 Appeal Review

Appeals are reviewed by a different moderator than the one who made the original decision. We will:
- Review the content in question
- Consider your explanation
- Make a determination within 7 days
- Notify you of the decision

### 6.3 Appeal Outcomes

After review, we may:
- Reverse the original action
- Uphold the original action
- Modify the original action

Our decision on appeal is final.
