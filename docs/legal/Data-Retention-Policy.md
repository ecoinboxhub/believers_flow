## 1. Introduction

### 1.1 Purpose

This Data Retention Policy explains how long we retain your data and how we delete it when it's no longer needed.

### 1.2 Principles

We follow these data retention principles:
- **Purpose limitation:** We only keep data as long as needed for its purpose
- **Data minimization:** We minimize the amount of data we retain
- **Storage limitation:** We delete data when it's no longer needed
- **Accuracy:** We keep data accurate and up to date

## 3. Data Deletion

### 3.1 User-Initiated Deletion

You can delete your data at any time:
- **Account deletion:** Settings > Profile > Delete Account
- **Content deletion:** Delete individual items
- **Export and delete:** Export data first, then delete

### 3.2 Account Deletion

When you delete your account:
- All personal information is deleted
- All user content is deleted
- All AI data is deleted
- All sync data is deleted
- Analytics data is anonymized
- Security logs are retained for 30 days, then deleted

### 3.3 Automated Deletion

Some data is automatically deleted:
- AI data: After 30 days
- Sync logs: After 30 days
- Analytics data: After 12 months
- Error logs: After 30 days

### 3.4 Backup Deletion

Data in backups is deleted when:
- The backup rotates (typically 30 days)
- You request deletion and we manually purge backups

## 4. Legal Requirements

### 4.1 Legal Obligations

We may retain data longer when required by law:
- **Tax records:** 7 years (varies by jurisdiction)
- **Financial records:** 7 years (varies by jurisdiction)
- **Legal claims:** Until statute of limitations expires

### 4.2 Business Needs

We may retain data longer for legitimate business needs:
- **Fraud prevention:** As long as necessary
- **Security investigations:** Until resolved
- **Legal proceedings:** Until resolved

### 4.3 Anonymization

When data is no longer needed in identifiable form, we may anonymize it for:
- Research and analysis
- Service improvement
- Statistical purposes
