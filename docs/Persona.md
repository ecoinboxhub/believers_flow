# User Persona - Believers Task Flow

## 1. "Christian Chris" (The Busy Professional)
- **Age**: 34
- **Role**: Software Engineer / Father of two.
- **Pain Point**: Finds it hard to maintain a prayer life during intense work weeks.
- **Goal**: Use a task manager that reminds him to "pray without ceasing" and helps him track his Bible reading progress.
- **App Usage**: Uses the "Prayer Balance" tracker to ensure he isn't neglecting his spiritual walk for work.

## 2. "Student Sarah" (The Intentional Disciple)
- **Age**: 21
- **Role**: University Student.
- **Pain Point**: Overwhelmed by academic tasks; often forgets her Bible study plan.
- **Goal**: A lightweight app that combines her study schedule with her chores.
- **App Usage**: Relies on the "Bible Study Planner" and the AI-generated activity suggestions to stay engaged with her faith.

## 3. "Elder Edward" (The Disciplined Senior)
- **Age**: 68
- **Role**: Retired / Church Volunteer.
- **Pain Point**: Traditional apps are too complex or "busy."
- **Goal**: A clean, simple interface with large icons and encouraging verses.
- **App Usage**: Enjoys the "Bible Verse Provider" and the simple category filters.
